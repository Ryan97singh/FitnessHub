Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a free trial? ▾

Yes! Walk in any time between 5am and 10pm for a free trial session. You can also WhatsApp us beforehand to book a slot and ensure a trainer is available for you.

What are your membership prices? ▾

We offer Monthly (₹2,500), Quarterly (₹6,500), and Yearly (₹18,000) plans. WhatsApp us for the latest offers and any ongoing discounts.

Do you have female trainers? ▾

Please WhatsApp us or call to confirm female trainer availability. We aim to accommodate all members comfortably.

Is there parking available? ▾

Yes, parking is available within the Hari Enclave complex. We are inside the Institute of Management & Technology campus, Sector 87.

Do you offer personal training? ▾

Absolutely. Personal training is available with both Sanchit and Devesh. Sessions are 60 minutes, tailored to your specific fitness goals.

Can I freeze my membership? ▾

Membership freeze policies depend on your plan. Please contact us on WhatsApp or visit the gym to discuss your specific situation.

What age group do you cater to? ▾

We welcome members from age 16 and above. Members under 18 should have parental consent. We have programs suitable for teens, adults, and seniors.

Is there a joining fee? ▾

Currently there is no separate joining fee — you just pay the membership price. WhatsApp us to confirm the latest pricing before visiting.
