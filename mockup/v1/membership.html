Membership FAQs

Can I upgrade my plan mid-cycle?▾

Yes, you can upgrade at any time. The remaining value of your current plan is typically credited toward the new one. WhatsApp us to arrange the upgrade.

Is there a registration/joining fee?▾

Currently there is no separate joining fee — you just pay the membership price directly. Confirm with the gym as this may change.

Can I freeze my membership?▾

Freeze options are available for longer plans (Quarterly and Yearly) in case of travel or injury. Contact the gym to discuss your situation.

Do you offer student or corporate discounts?▾

We may offer special rates for students and corporate groups. WhatsApp us to inquire about current promotions.
