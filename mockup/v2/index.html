FAQs

Do you offer a free trial? ▾

Yes! Walk in any time between 5am and 10pm for a free trial session. You can also WhatsApp us beforehand to book a slot.

What are your membership prices? ▾

Monthly ₹2,500 · Quarterly ₹6,500 · Yearly ₹18,000. WhatsApp us for the latest offers and discounts.

Do you have female trainers? ▾

Please WhatsApp us or call to confirm female trainer availability. We aim to accommodate all members comfortably.

Is there parking available? ▾

Yes, parking is available within the Hari Enclave complex, inside the Institute of Management & Technology campus, Sector 87.

Do you offer personal training? ▾

Yes. Personal training is available with both Sanchit and Devesh. 60-minute sessions tailored to your specific goals.

Can I freeze my membership? ▾

Freeze options are available for Quarterly and Yearly plans. Contact us on WhatsApp to discuss your situation.

What age group do you cater to? ▾

We welcome members from age 16 and above. Programs suitable for teens, adults, and seniors.

Is there a joining fee? ▾

Currently no separate joining fee — you just pay the membership price. WhatsApp us to confirm the latest pricing.
